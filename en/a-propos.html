Family business for over 50 years.

A regional company established since 1971, we operate five convenience stores in Val-d'Or, Rouyn-Noranda and Louvicourt. We offer a full range of services including ready-to-eat food, grocery items, lottery, fuel, as well as car wash and dog wash services.

Open 24 hours a day, we maintain high standards of quality and service. Recognized as a regional landmark, we are a proactive company, constantly innovating to meet our customers' needs.

Our history and key milestones

It all started with Raymond Beauséjour, a Shell Canada trainer who acquired the "Pinto" station at 202, 3e Avenue in Val-d'Or in 1970. His son Érick joined the company in 1994 and has been growing it ever since.

Today, Groupe Beauséjour operates five convenience stores in Abitibi, with a unique positioning that sets it apart from traditional retail through its warm and human approach.
