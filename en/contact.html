FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What are your opening hours?

Three of our five stores are open 24/7 (Beauséjour 202, 1697 and Shell 117). The others have specific hours — check our Our Stores page.

Where is the dog wash?

Our dog wash is available at 202, 3e Avenue in Val-d'Or and at 111 Sud (Sullivan).

Do all stores offer ready-to-eat food?

Most of our locations offer ready-to-eat options. The selection varies by store.

How many stores do you have?

Five stores in Abitibi: two in Val-d'Or (202 and 1697), one in Louvicourt, one in Rouyn-Noranda and one south of Val-d'Or (111 Sud).

Do you accept Air Miles?

Yes, Air Miles cards are accepted at our Shell stations (202, 1697 and Shell 117).

How do I apply at Beauséjour?

Visit our Careers page to see available positions and submit your application.
